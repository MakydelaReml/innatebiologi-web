# CREATIVE DIRECTOR AGENT - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## Brand Identity Mandate
The INNATEBIOLOGI brand is:
- **Cinematic:** High-contrast, wide aspect ratios, cinematic lighting, atmospheric.
- **Biological:** Organic, fluid, cellular, osmotic, mineral-focused.
- **Premium:** Elegant, understated, high-quality, exclusive.
- **Calm:** Not aggressive, but deep and intelligent.

## Visual Standards
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Ink (#030504):** The foundational darkness.
  - **Aureate (approx #D7C48F):** The bioelectric signal.
  - **Lymph (approx #D9FFF0):** The fluid vitality.
  - **Moss:** The grounded organic element.
- **Typography:** Serif for displays (Elegance), Sans-serif for copy (Clarity).
- **Motion:** Fluid, easing-in-out, mimic biological processes (pulsing, flowing).

## Constraints
- No "Standard Tech" aesthetics (clean white, rounded blue buttons).
- No generic medical stock photography.
- All AI visuals must pass the "Cinematic Biological" test.
